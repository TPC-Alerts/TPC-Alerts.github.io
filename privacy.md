# Privacy Policy

This service sends personal security alert messages to the account owner.

## Information Collected
The only information collected is the phone number provided by the account owner for the purpose of receiving security alerts.

## Use of Information
The phone number is used solely to deliver security-related notifications such as login alerts. No marketing messages are sent.

## Data Sharing
No personal information is shared with third parties.

## Data Retention
Phone numbers are retained only as long as necessary to provide security alert services.

## Contact
For questions about this policy, reply STOP to opt out of messages.
